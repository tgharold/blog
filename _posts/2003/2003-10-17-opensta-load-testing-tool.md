[OpenSTA](http://opensta.org/) is a decent tool for testing out websites.  The learning curve for it is a bit steep until you manage to figure out exactly what bits are which and how to do a simple test.  However, it was good enough to test a web application that I wrote at work that depends on a value passed back and forth to maintain session state (rather then depending on a server-issued session cookie).
